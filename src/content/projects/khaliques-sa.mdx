## Problem

Fashion-conscious men needed a streamlined mobile shopping experience with curated menswear collections, accurate sizing information, and reliable order fulfillment.

## Approach

Built an iOS e-commerce app in Swift with a focus on product presentation and user experience. Implemented image caching with Kingfisher for smooth browsing, Stripe integration for secure checkout, and Core Data for wishlist and cart persistence.

## Key Features

- Curated men's fashion collections
- High-resolution product imagery with zoom
- Detailed size guides and fit recommendations
- Wishlist and shopping cart with offline support
- Secure checkout via Stripe
- Order tracking and history
- Push notifications for new arrivals and promotions

## Result

Published on the App Store, providing a refined mobile shopping experience for men's fashion with seamless checkout and order management.
