## Problem

People in recovery from addiction needed a dedicated space to connect romantically and socially with others who understand and support sobriety, free from the pressures of traditional dating platforms.

## Approach

Built an iOS app in Swift that combines dating features with community support. Integrated Firebase for real-time messaging and user presence. Focused on privacy, safety features, and creating an inclusive environment for various recovery paths.

## Key Features

- Sober-focused dating profiles and matching
- Community forums and support groups
- Real-time messaging with safety features
- Milestone tracking and sobriety badges
- Event listings for sober activities
- Anonymous support mode

## Result

Published on the App Store, providing a safe, judgment-free platform for individuals in recovery to build meaningful connections and support networks.
