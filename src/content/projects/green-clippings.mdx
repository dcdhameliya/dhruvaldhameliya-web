## Problem

Miami homeowners needed a convenient way to schedule and manage professional lawn care services, while landscapers needed a reliable platform to find clients and manage bookings.

## Approach

Built an iOS app in Swift with two-sided marketplace functionality. Integrated MapKit for service area coverage, Stripe for payments, and real-time booking management. Focused on Miami's local market with seasonal lawn care needs.

## Key Features

- Browse and book verified lawn care professionals
- Service area coverage with MapKit integration
- Instant quotes and service scheduling
- Secure payments via Stripe
- Job tracking and notifications
- Rating and review system
- Before/after photo uploads

## Result

Published on the App Store, streamlining lawn care bookings for Miami homeowners and providing landscapers with a steady stream of verified jobs.
